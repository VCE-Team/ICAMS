ICAMS 2024 Conference Invitation

Greetings from ICAMS 2024, Hyderabad, India.

It’s our immense pleasure to invite you cordially to submit your research manuscripts for the upcoming conference; 6th International Congress on Advances in Mechanical Sciences (ICAMS 2024). The conference is scheduled to be held at Vardhaman College of Engineering, Hyderabad, India during 26th–27th July 2024.

IMPORTANT DATES:

  • Full paper submission: 30th March, 2024
  • Notification of Paper Acceptance: 20th April 2024
  • Deadline for Camera-Ready Paper Submission: 30th April 2024
  • Conference ICAMS 2020: 26-27 July 2024
  • Early Registration Deadline: 04th May 2024

PUBLICATION:

All the accepted papers after peer review will be published either SCI or Scopus Indexed Journals. Authors are requested to prepare manuscript on the originality of their work and Plagiarism should be below 15%.

Please visit our website: ICAMS 2024

Link for submission of papers will be updated soon.

It is highly appreciated if you kindly forward this conference information to your research colleagues and friends.
