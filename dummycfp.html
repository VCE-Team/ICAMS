ICAMS 2024 Conference Invitation

Greetings from ICAMS 2024, Hyderabad, India.

It’s our immense pleasure to invite you cordially to submit your research manuscripts for the upcoming conference; 6th International Congress on Advances in Mechanical Sciences (ICAMS 2024). The conference is scheduled to be held at Vardhaman College of Engineering, Hyderabad, India during 26th–27th July 2024.

IMPORTANT DATES:

  • Full paper submission 15th June, 2024
  • Notification of Paper Acceptance 25th June, 2024
  • Deadline for Camera-Ready Paper Submission 30th June, 2024
  • Conference ICAMS2024 26-27 July 2024
  • Early Registration Deadline 25th June, 2024

All the accepted papers after peer review will be published in Scopus Indexed Journals.
Authors are requested to prepare manuscript on the originality of their work and Plagiarism should be below 10%.
